## 1. Public Access
### Overview
Public access means anyone can explore our website without needing an account.

### How to Access the Site
To access the site, follow these steps:
1. Open your web browser (like Chrome or Firefox).
2. Type in the website address: www.cpa-virtual.com and press Enter.
3. Wait for the page to load. You will see the main page of CPA Virtual.

### Navigating the Main Page
Once you are on the main page, you can see different sections. Look for:
- The logo at the top, which takes you back to the main page.
- Menu options on the side or top, guiding you to different areas of the site.

## 2. Authentication
### What is Authentication?
Authentication is how we confirm you are who you say you are before allowing access to secure areas.

### How to Create an Account
Follow these easy steps to create your account:
1. Click on the 'Sign Up' button on the main page.
2. Fill out the form with your name, email, and a password you will remember.
3. Click 'Submit'. You will receive a confirmation email.

### How to Log In
To log in, do the following:
1. Click on the 'Log In' button.
2. Enter your email and password.
3. Click 'Submit'. You will now be logged into your profile.

### How to Reset Your Password
If you forget your password, you can reset it by:
1. Clicking on the 'Forgot Password?' link on the log-in page.
2. Entering your email and following the instructions in the email you receive.

## 3. User Profile
### Viewing Your Profile
After logging in, you can view your profile by:
1. Clicking on 'My Profile' in the menu.
2. Here you can see your information.

### Editing Your Information
To edit your information:
1. Click 'Edit Profile'.
2. Update your details, then click 'Save'.

### Security Tips
- Never share your password with others.
- Keep personal information private.

## 4. Workshops
### Overview of Workshops
Workshops provide hands-on learning experiences. You can join various topics.

### How to Register for a Workshop
Sign up for workshops by:
1. Selecting a workshop from the list.
2. Clicking 'Register'.

### What to Expect
Expect friendly instructors and supportive participants! 

## 5. Activities
